### [CVE-2026-31431 Copy Fail: A 4-Byte Kernel Write That Escapes Containers](https://devops-daily.com/posts/copy-fail-cve-2026-31431-linux-container-escape)

![CVE-2026-31431 Copy Fail post](/images/posts/copy-fail-cve-2026-31431-linux-container-escape.png)

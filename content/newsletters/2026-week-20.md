## Blog Posts

### [Dirty Frag (CVE-2026-43284 + CVE-2026-43500): Local Root on Every Major Linux Distro](https://devops-daily.com/posts/dirty-frag-cve-2026-43284-linux-root-escalation)

### [Mini Shai-Hulud: PyTorch Lightning Just Stole Your CI Secrets](https://devops-daily.com/posts/mini-shai-hulud-pytorch-lightning-supply-chain-attack)

![Mini Shai-Hulud: PyTorch Lightning Just Stole Your CI Secrets](/images/posts/mini-shai-hulud-pytorch-lightning-supply-chain-attack.png)

A supply chain worm slipped into PyTorch Lightning on April 30 and started sweeping CI secrets from anyone who installed the bad versions. Check your pipelines and rotate anything that touched it.

### [CVE-2026-3854: A Single git push Owned GitHub](https://devops-daily.com/posts/github-cve-2026-3854-git-push-rce)

![CVE-2026-3854: A Single git push Owned GitHub](/images/posts/github-cve-2026-3854-git-push-rce.png)

Turns out a single semicolon in a git push option was enough to run code on GitHub.com's backend. The writeup is a great read on how small input mistakes turn into massive blast radius bugs.

### [Istio Traffic Management: Routing, Retries, and Circuit Breaking](https://devops-daily.com/posts/istio-traffic-management-routing-retries-circuit-breaking)
